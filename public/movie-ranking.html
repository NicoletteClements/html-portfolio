My top 3 movies of all times

Pretty Woman

This has been my favourite move since teenage years
A true fairytale with my favourite 2 actors, Richard Gere & Julia Roberts

The Other Women

One of my more recent favourites as it has Cameron Diaz and is a mix of incredible humor, lifes realities, unexpected real friendships and a fairytale ending

The Proposal

One of my favourite actresses, Sandra Bullock, with an unexpected and unpredictable twist
